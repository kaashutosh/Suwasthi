Privacy policy

At suwasthi.com, your privacy is our top priority. We are committed to protecting your personal, payment, and order information. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data. We do not share your information with any unauthorized third-party systems.

1. User Information and Privacy

We take stringent measures to protect the confidentiality, security, and integrity of all information you share with us.

  • Data Security:
    • We use Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology to encrypt your credit card number and other personal information, ensuring secure data transmission.
    • Only authorized employees with specific duties can access your information. Violations may result in disciplinary action, including possible termination and legal consequences.
  • Commitment to Privacy:
    • Your data is stored securely and handled with utmost care to prevent unauthorized access or misuse.

2. Secure Shopping at suwasthi.com

When shopping with us, you can rest assured that your personal and payment information is secure. We utilize:

  • Encryption technology.
  • Digital certificates.
  • Secure commerce servers.
  • Authentication protocols.

These measures ensure that your online transactions are protected at every step.

3. User Registration

To provide you with the best shopping experience, we collect and store certain information when you register or make a purchase:

  • Information Collected:
    • Name, phone number, email address, billing and shipping addresses, and payment details.
  • Purpose of Collection:
    • To fulfill your orders and provide access to your account.
    • To contact you regarding your orders, if necessary.
    • To notify you about new services, promotions, or offers (if you opt-in).
  • Email Communication:
    • Emails are sent only to users who have opted in or made purchases. You can unsubscribe at any time.
  • Additional Records:
    • We may keep records of your past purchases, returns, and preferences to improve your shopping experience.
