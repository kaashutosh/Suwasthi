Founded in 2017, Suwasthi has become a trusted name in the world of Ayurvedic wellness. As a completely bootstrapped and profitable company, we proudly ship over 90,000 orders each month. Our current team of 500 passionate members is our backbone, and together, we are committed to a bold mission: To help 10 crore people manage lifestyle-related health challenges with the age-old benefits of Ayurveda by 2030.

Joining Suwasthi means you're not just part of a company; you're part of a movement that contributes to a healthier and happier world.
