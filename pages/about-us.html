Our Story

Our journey began in 2017 with a simple belief: wellness should be natural, effective, and accessible to everyone. With over 2 million products sold, we’ve had the privilege of transforming millions of lives.

Our name, derived from the Sanskrit words “Su” (good) and “Swasthi” (health or well-being), reflects our commitment to promoting good health and transparency in everything we do.

We’ve combined Ayurveda, the world’s oldest healing wisdom, with modern science to create healthy formulas that fit seamlessly into today’s modern lifestyle. Our ingredients are carefully sourced from India’s lush herb jungles and Himalayan ranges, ensuring purity and respect for nature at every step.

No shortcuts. No hidden ingredients. Just pure, high-quality supplements thoughtfully designed to work in harmony with your body.

By uniting Ayurveda’s timeless wisdom with the precision of modern science, we’re redefining wellness for the modern world.
